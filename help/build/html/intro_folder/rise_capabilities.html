Capabilities of RISE¶

  • switching parameters

    • endogenous switching
  • optimal policy (with and without switching)
    • discretion
    • commitment
    • loose commitment
    • optimized simple rules

  • Deterministic simulation

  • Stochastic simulation

  • higher-order perturbations
